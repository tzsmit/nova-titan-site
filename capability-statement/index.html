Company Overview

Nova Titan Systems LLC is a SBA-Certified HUBZone Small Business and Division 27 low-voltage infrastructure contractor headquartered in Lubbock, Texas. We provide structured cabling, network infrastructure, wireless access point support, security camera cabling, access control cabling, and field technology services for commercial, education, healthcare, government, and industrial clients across West Texas and the broader Texas market. Nova Titan provides field execution, project coordination, and closeout support. SAM.gov active. UEI: CZ8BLZMSKZ66 • CAGE: 170W6.
