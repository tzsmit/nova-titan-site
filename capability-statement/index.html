Company Overview

Nova Titan Systems LLC provides IT infrastructure support, cybersecurity services, cloud security, field technical services, telecommunications, and ICS/OT support for government and commercial clients. Strategically positioned in Lubbock, Texas, we deliver on-site services throughout West Texas and nationwide remote support capabilities.

As a Small Business and Minority-Owned enterprise, we are positioned to serve as both prime contractor and subcontractor. We are micro-purchase ready and accept credit card payments for rapid procurement.
