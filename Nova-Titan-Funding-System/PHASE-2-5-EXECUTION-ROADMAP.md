## üö® Risk Mitigation & Contingencies

### **Risk 1: SAM.gov Activation Delay**
**Impact:** Government contracting delayed  
**Mitigation:** Focus on CDFI loans, grants, and supplier diversity while SAM.gov is pending  
**Contingency:** Pursue state/local government contracts that may not require SAM.gov

### **Risk 2: Funding Applications Rejected**
**Impact:** Cash flow constraints  
**Mitigation:** Diversify funding sources (CDFI, grants, SBA, private investors)  
**Contingency:** Bootstrap revenue growth, pursue alternative lenders

### **Risk 3: Website/Automation Costs Exceed Budget**
**Impact:** Phase 3-4 delayed  
**Mitigation:** Prioritize highest-ROI automations (billing, CRM), DIY website work  
**Contingency:** Phased implementation, use free/low-cost tools initially

### **Risk 4: Client Acquisition Slower Than Projected**
**Impact:** Revenue targets missed  
**Mitigation:** Aggressive networking, referral programs, strategic partnerships  
**Contingency:** Adjust financial projections, extend runway with part-time work or consulting
