## üé® Blog Post Template

### **Front Matter (Required):**
```yaml
---
layout: post
title: "Your SEO-Optimized Title Here"
date: 2025-XX-XX 10:00:00 -0600
categories: [Technology, Small Business, West Texas]
tags: [specific, relevant, tags, here]
excerpt: "Compelling 1-2 sentence summary that appears in social shares and search results."
author: "Traivone Smith"
image: /assets/img/blog/post-slug.jpg  # Optional featured image
---
```

### **Structure:**
1. **Introduction (150-200 words)**
   - Hook with local context or business problem
   - State what reader will learn
   - Mention Nova Titan's expertise

2. **Body (700-900 words)**
   - 3-5 main sections with H2 headings
   - Use bullet points, numbered lists for scannability
   - Include real examples, case studies, local context
   - Add statistics/data where relevant
   - Use **bold** for emphasis, not just for keywords

3. **West Texas Context (100-150 words)**
   - Local business challenges
   - Industry-specific considerations (oil & gas, agriculture, healthcare)
   - Regional examples (Lubbock, Midland, Odessa, Amarillo)

4. **How Nova Titan Can Help (150-200 words)**
   - Relevant services for this topic
   - Pricing transparency (starting at $X)
   - Service area mention
   - Internal links to service pages

5. **Conclusion & CTA (100 words)**
   - Summarize key takeaways
   - Strong call-to-action
   - Contact info (phone, email, book now link)

6. **Author Bio (50 words)**
   - Brief Nova Titan description
   - Credentials (OCI certified, Computer Science student)
   - Service area

### **SEO Optimization Checklist:**
- [ ] Title 50-60 characters, includes primary keyword
- [ ] Excerpt 120-160 characters (meta description)
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] H2/H3 headings include keywords naturally
- [ ] 3-5 internal links to service/plan pages
- [ ] 1-2 external links to authoritative sources
- [ ] Alt text for all images
- [ ] URL slug is short and keyword-rich (/blog/managed-it-west-texas)
- [ ] Categories: 2-3 max (Technology, Small Business, West Texas, etc.)
- [ ] Tags: 4-6 specific tags
- [ ] Featured image: 1200x630px for social sharing
