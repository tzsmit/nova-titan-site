Traivone Smith

CEO & Founder, Nova Titan Systems LLC

I founded Nova Titan Systems in 2020 with a clear vision: bring enterprise-grade technology solutions to West Texas while building a company capable of competing on the national stage.

As a Computer Science student with an Information Security focus and Oracle Cloud Infrastructure certification, I've combined academic rigor with real-world field experience to create a multi-division technology company serving everyone from homeowners to Fortune 500 enterprises.

What started as a solo IT service has evolved into Nova Titan Systems™ (Infrastructure), Nova Titan Security™ (Cybersecurity), and Nova Titan Intelligence™ (AI/Data)—three specialized divisions delivering comprehensive technology solutions.

OCI Foundations Certified CS Student (InfoSec) Lubbock Chamber Member

Nova Titan Systems was founded on a simple belief: West Texas deserves enterprise-grade technology solutions without compromise. We're not just a service provider—we're building a technology company that competes nationally while staying rooted in our community.
