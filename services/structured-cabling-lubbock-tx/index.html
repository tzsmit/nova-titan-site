Scope & Deliverables

Every structured cabling project is scoped from a site survey. We provide fixed-price proposals with clear deliverables and deliver certified documentation at closeout.

  • Site Survey — We walk the space, assess conduit needs, identify telecom room placement, and scope runs before pricing.
  • Installation — Clean pulls, labeled runs, proper bend radius, secured pathways.
  • Termination — Patch panels, keystones, and faceplates installed and dressed neatly.
  • Certification — Fluke DSX cable certification with pass/fail documentation.
  • Closeout — As-built documentation, port maps, and labeled infrastructure delivered at completion.
