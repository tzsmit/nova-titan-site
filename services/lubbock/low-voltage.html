How long does a typical cabling installation take?

Small office installations (10-20 drops) typically complete in 1-2 days. Mid-size deployments (50-100 drops) take 3-5 days. Large commercial or government facilities require custom timelines based on scope. We provide detailed project schedules during the consultation phase.

What's the difference between CAT5e, CAT6, and CAT6A cabling?

CAT5e supports 1 Gbps up to 100 meters (sufficient for most office networks). CAT6 supports 10 Gbps up to 55 meters (better for high-bandwidth applications). CAT6A supports 10 Gbps up to 100 meters (future-proof for PoE++ and high-density environments). We recommend CAT6A for new commercial installations.
