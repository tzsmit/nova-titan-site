Permian Basin Low-Voltage Subcontractor

The Permian Basin is one of the fastest-growing commercial construction markets in Texas, driven by energy sector expansion, healthcare development, and government investment. Nova Titan Systems provides Division 27 and Division 28 low-voltage subcontracting for general contractors executing projects in Midland, Odessa, and surrounding communities including Andrews, Pecos, Monahans, Big Spring, and Seminole.

Our Lubbock headquarters is approximately 120 miles from Midland-Odessa, enabling same-day mobilization and reducing travel overhead compared to contractors dispatching from Houston or DFW. CEO Traivone Smith oversees every project personally, ensuring Division 27/28 scope is delivered on time, within budget, and to NFPA, TIA/EIA, and BICSI standards.

Nova Titan is actively registered on SAM.gov (UEI CZ8BLZMSKZ66, CAGE 170W6) and is self-certified minority-owned, qualifying for diversity subcontracting goals on federal, state, and municipal projects across the Permian Basin.
