Lubbock's Commercial Low-Voltage Subcontractor

Nova Titan Systems is headquartered in Lubbock, Texas, providing Division 27 and Division 28 low-voltage subcontracting to general contractors building commercial, retail, healthcare, education, and government facilities across the South Plains and West Texas corridor.

Our Lubbock-based crews deliver structured cabling (CAT6/6A copper and fiber optic), fire alarm rough-in coordination, access control infrastructure, security camera cabling, Wi-Fi access point deployment, and AV/PA system wiring. We work alongside GCs from ground-up construction through tenant improvement, maintaining NFPA, TIA/EIA, and BICSI standards on every job. Regulated fire alarm programming, testing, and tagging is performed by licensed Texas FA subcontractors engaged at award.

Because our dispatch center is local, Lubbock and West Texas projects benefit from same-day mobilization, reduced travel costs, and a team familiar with local code enforcement, inspection processes, and AHJ requirements. Our 500-mile dispatch radius also covers Midland, Odessa, Amarillo, Abilene, and the Permian Basin.
