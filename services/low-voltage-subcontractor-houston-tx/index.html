Houston Division 27/28 Low-Voltage Subcontracting

Nova Titan Systems extends its Division 27/28 subcontracting capabilities into the Houston metropolitan area, one of the fastest-growing commercial construction markets in the United States. Houston's booming healthcare, energy, logistics, and retail construction sectors demand reliable low-voltage subcontractors who can deliver structured cabling, fire alarm rough-in coordination, access control, and security camera infrastructure on aggressive schedules.

From our Texas base, our crews deploy to the Houston metro for projects ranging from multi-story office towers and hospital expansions to distribution centers, retail build-outs, and government renovations. We perform CAT6/6A copper cabling, single-mode and multi-mode fiber, fire alarm rough-in coordination, access control reader installation, IP camera infrastructure, and Wi-Fi access point deployment — all certified to TIA/EIA, BICSI, and NFPA standards. Regulated FA programming, testing, and tagging is performed by licensed Texas FA subcontractors engaged at award.

Houston general contractors benefit from Nova Titan's self-certification and SAM.gov registration when pursuing projects with subcontracting requirements, federal subcontracting requirements, or state subcontracting requirements. Our CAGE Code (170W6) and primary NAICS 238210 make us an easy addition to your approved subcontractor list.
