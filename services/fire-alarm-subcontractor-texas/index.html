Commercial Fire Alarm Rough-In Across Texas

Nova Titan Systems delivers Division 28 fire alarm rough-in and coordination services for general contractors building commercial, institutional, and government facilities throughout Texas. Based in Lubbock with a 500-mile dispatch radius, we cover West Texas, Houston, Dallas-Fort Worth, and points between.

Our scope of work includes conduit and raceway installation, device location coordination, pathway design support, sleeve and core penetration coordination, and adjacent low-voltage scope tied to the fire alarm system. All regulated work — including device programming, end-to-end testing, AHJ inspection sign-off, and tagging — is performed by properly licensed Texas fire alarm subcontractors engaged at the time of award. This partnership model ensures full code compliance under Texas Insurance Code while giving GCs a single point of accountability for the rough-in package.

As a SAM.gov-registered, minority-owned small business (NAICS 238210, CAGE 170W6), Nova Titan is qualified for federal, state, and local government projects, Davis-Bacon prevailing wage work, and micro-purchase and simplified acquisition procurements.

What We Deliver

Conduit & Pathway Rough-In

EMT, MC cable runs, sleeves, and core penetrations sized for FA device circuits and SLC loops per drawings.

Device Location Coordination

Field coordination of pull stations, horn/strobes, smoke and heat detectors, and duct detectors against architectural and MEP plans.

Licensed FA Partner Coordination

Engagement and scheduling of properly licensed Texas FA subcontractors at award for all regulated programming, testing, and tagging.

Adjacent LV & Closeout Support

Low-voltage scope captured alongside the FA package, plus closeout documentation, as-builts, and AHJ coordination support.
