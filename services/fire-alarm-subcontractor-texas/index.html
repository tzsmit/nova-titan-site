Commercial Fire Alarm Installation in Texas

Nova Titan Systems delivers Division 28 fire alarm subcontracting for general contractors building commercial, institutional, and government facilities throughout Texas. Based in Lubbock with a 500-mile dispatch radius, we cover West Texas, Houston, Dallas-Fort Worth, and points between.

Our fire alarm services include new-construction installation, device mounting and wiring, fire alarm control panel (FACP) installation, notification appliance circuit (NAC) wiring, initiating device circuit (IDC) installation, and design-assist coordination with the project engineer of record. We work with all major panel manufacturers and follow NFPA 72 standards for every installation.

As a SAM.gov-registered, MBE-certified small business (NAICS 238210, CAGE 170W6), Nova Titan is qualified for federal, state, and local government fire alarm projects, Davis-Bacon prevailing wage work, and micro-purchase and simplified acquisition procurements.

Fire Alarm Services We Provide

Panel Installation

Fire alarm control panel (FACP) mounting, wiring, and programming support for addressable and conventional systems.

Device Wiring

Design-Assist

Pre-inspection verification, point-to-point testing, and AHJ inspection support to ensure first-pass approval.
