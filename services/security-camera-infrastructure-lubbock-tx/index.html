What We Do

We install the physical infrastructure — cabling, mounts, racks, and pathways. Camera programming, VMS configuration, and monitoring setup are outside our scope unless subcontracted to a qualified integrator.

  • Camera Cabling — CAT6 runs from camera positions to NVR or PoE switch locations, labeled and documented.
  • PoE Infrastructure — Switch shelf, mounting, and cable routing to support IP camera power over Ethernet.
  • Camera Mounting — Brackets, junction boxes, and physical mount installation for indoor and outdoor cameras.
  • NVR Rack Integration — NVR placement, cable dressing, and power routing in equipment rack or cabinet.
  • Closeout — Port maps, cable labels, and documentation delivered at project close.
