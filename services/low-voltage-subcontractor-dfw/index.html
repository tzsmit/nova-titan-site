DFW Division 27/28 Low-Voltage Subcontracting

The Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex is one of the nation's most active commercial construction markets, with continuous new builds in corporate office, mixed-use, healthcare, education, retail, and government sectors. Nova Titan Systems provides Division 27 and Division 28 low-voltage subcontracting to general contractors across the DFW metro, from downtown Dallas through Fort Worth, Arlington, Frisco, Plano, McKinney, Denton, and surrounding areas.

Our DFW services cover structured cabling (CAT6/6A copper and fiber optic), access control reader and panel installation, security camera cabling and PoE infrastructure, Wi-Fi access point deployment, AV/PA system wiring, and Division 28 fire alarm rough-in coordination. All regulated FA work — including device programming, end-to-end testing, AHJ sign-off, and tagging — is performed by properly licensed Texas fire alarm subcontractors engaged at award. Every installation is performed to TIA/EIA and BICSI standards with Fluke DSX cable certification available on request.

DFW general contractors working on TxDOT, City of Dallas, Tarrant County, DISD, or federal projects benefit from Nova Titan's SAM.gov registration (UEI CZ8BLZMSKZ66, CAGE 170W6), minority-owned self-certification, and small disadvantaged business status. Our NAICS 238210 classification and Davis-Bacon readiness make us an effective subcontracting partner for projects with socioeconomic participation goals.
