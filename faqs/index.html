Emergency Support

Do you provide 24/7 emergency support?

Yes! Emergency cybersecurity support is available 24/7.

What qualifies as an emergency:

  • Active security breach or malware infection
  • Complete network outage affecting business operations
  • Suspected data theft or unauthorized access
  • Ransomware attacks
  • Critical system compromises

Emergency Contact: Call (806) 370-0624 directly - calls are monitored 24/7.

What should I do if I think I've been hacked?

Immediate Steps:

  1. Stay calm - Don't panic or shut everything down immediately
  2. Call (806) 370-0624 for immediate guidance
  3. Document what happened - Note any unusual behavior
  4. Isolate affected systems - Disconnect from internet if advised
  5. Don't delete anything - Evidence may be needed

What NOT to do:

  • Don't pay ransomware demands without consultation
  • Don't use compromised systems for sensitive work
  • Don't attempt DIY fixes that might destroy evidence
