Emergency Support

What constitutes a cybersecurity emergency?

Cybersecurity Emergencies Include:

  • Active ransomware or malware attacks
  • Suspected data breaches or unauthorized access
  • Business-critical systems down due to security issues
  • Confirmed network intrusions
  • DDoS attacks affecting operations
  • Loss of access to critical systems or data

Response Protocol:

  1. Call (806) 370-0624 immediately
  2. Do NOT turn off affected systems unless instructed
  3. Disconnect from internet if safe to do so
  4. Document what happened and when
  5. Preserve any evidence or suspicious emails
