Technical Questions

What cabling types do you install?

Copper Cabling:

  • Cat5e — standard commercial installations
  • Cat6 — most new commercial projects
  • Cat6a — high-performance and PoE+ environments

Fiber Optic:

  • Single-mode and multi-mode fiber
  • Backbone runs between floors and buildings
  • Fiber-to-the-desk for high-bandwidth requirements

All installations include Fluke DSX-series certification testing and full documentation.

What network equipment do you work with?

Access Points & Wireless: Ubiquiti/UniFi, Cisco Meraki, Aruba, Cisco Small Business

Firewalls: Fortinet FortiGate (Fortinet Certified Associate)

Switching: Cisco, HPE/Aruba, Ubiquiti, Juniper

POS Systems: Clover, Square, Toast, Lightspeed

Do you handle security camera installation?

We provide security camera infrastructure — the cabling and physical mounting of camera hardware. This includes:

  • Structured cabling runs to camera locations
  • PoE switch installation for camera power
  • Physical camera mounting and bracket installation
  • Conduit and pathway systems

Note: Camera programming and monitoring system configuration is performed by our licensed subcontractor partners. We coordinate the full installation for a seamless experience.
