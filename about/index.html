About Nova Titan Systems

Nova Titan Systems LLC is a Black-owned, minority-owned small business delivering enterprise-grade IT infrastructure, cybersecurity, and field engineering services to government agencies and commercial clients. Founded in Lubbock, Texas, we combine deep technical expertise with real-world execution.

We exist to bridge the gap between enterprise capability and small business agility—bringing technical precision, security-first design, and on-the-ground reliability to organizations that demand mission-critical performance.

Founder Story: Traivone Smith

Built from experience, driven by purpose

Traivone Smith founded Nova Titan Systems in 2024 after years of working inside the systems that power businesses, schools, banks, and industrial facilities across Texas. He didn't start with venture capital or a team. He started with a laptop, a toolbag, and a commitment to build something that delivers real value.

As a Black technologist from West Texas, Traivone saw firsthand the gap between what businesses need and what they actually get from IT vendors. Too often, organizations settle for cookie-cutter solutions, slow response times, and surface-level security. He built Nova Titan Systems to deliver what he would expect if he were the client: technical precision, security-first design, and follow-through that matters.

Background & Expertise:

  • Network engineering and infrastructure deployment across enterprise environments
  • Cybersecurity implementation, cloud hardening, and identity management (AWS/Azure)
  • Industrial and OT network systems for manufacturing, energy, and critical infrastructure
  • Field engineering and technical deployments for banks, schools, retail, and government facilities
  • Federal contracting and small business development

Traivone's mission is simple: build a technology company that creates generational ownership, brings enterprise-grade capability to underserved markets, and proves that minority-owned businesses can compete at the highest levels of technical delivery.

"Nova Titan Systems exists because communities deserve better technology infrastructure, businesses deserve reliable partners, and the next generation deserves to see what ownership looks like."

— Traivone Smith, Founder & CEO
