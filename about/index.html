About Nova Titan Systems

Nova Titan Systems LLC is a Black-owned, minority-owned small business providing IT infrastructure, cybersecurity, and field engineering services to federal, state, local, and commercial clients.

Based in Lubbock, TX and serving clients nationwide, we deliver enterprise-grade capability with small-business agility.

Founder: Traivone Smith

Traivone Smith founded Nova Titan Systems after years working inside the systems that power banks, schools, retail, and industrial facilities across Texas. He started with a laptop, a toolbag, and a commitment to build a business that delivers work the right way.

As a Black technologist from West Texas, he saw how often organizations receive slow support, cookie-cutter IT solutions, and surface-level security. He built Nova Titan Systems to change that—bringing enterprise discipline, security-first design, and real-world execution to every project.

  • Cybersecurity, cloud hardening & identity security (AWS/Azure)
  • Industrial/OT networking for manufacturing & critical infrastructure
  • Field engineering deployments across commercial & government environments
