About Nova Titan Systems

Nova Titan Systems LLC is a Black-owned IT contractor on a mission to bridge the digital divide through federal, state, and local government IT infrastructure serving underserved communities.

Based in rural West Texas and serving communities nationwide, we connect veterans, students, and families to the technology infrastructure they deserve—one government facility at a time.

Founder: Traivone Smith

Traivone Smith founded Nova Titan Systems after years working inside the systems that power banks, schools, retail, and industrial facilities across Texas. As a Black technologist from rural West Texas, he witnessed firsthand how lack of digital infrastructure limits opportunity in underserved communities.

He saw veterans in rural areas struggling with unreliable telehealth connections, small-town schools lacking proper cybersecurity, and government facilities with outdated infrastructure. He built Nova Titan Systems to change that—bringing enterprise-grade IT to the communities and government agencies that need it most.

Expertise Includes:

  • Network engineering & enterprise infrastructure
  • Cybersecurity, cloud hardening & identity security (AWS/Azure)
  • Industrial/OT networking for manufacturing & critical infrastructure
  • Government facility deployments (federal, state, local agencies)
  • Digital equity initiatives and rural broadband infrastructure
