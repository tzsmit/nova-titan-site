Founder: Traivone Smith

Traivone Smith founded Nova Titan Systems after years working inside the systems that power banks, schools, retail, and industrial facilities across Texas. As a professional technologist from rural West Texas, he built a company focused on delivering enterprise-quality IT services to businesses of all sizes.

With deep experience in network engineering, cybersecurity, and infrastructure deployment, Traivone built Nova Titan Systems to provide reliable, professional IT services with personal attention and accountability. Every project is handled with the same level of care, whether it's a small business network or a multi-site enterprise deployment.
