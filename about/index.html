About Nova Titan Systems

Built on hands-on experience and a commitment to reliable project delivery

Nova Titan Systems LLC was founded by Traivone Smith to deliver dependable Division 27/28 subcontracting services to general contractors, government agencies, and commercial facility owners. The company combines technical expertise in low-voltage systems with a hands-on approach to project management and field execution.

"We show up, we deliver clean work, and we close out on schedule. That is the standard at Nova Titan."

— Traivone Smith, CEO

Based in Lubbock, Texas, Nova Titan Systems serves the West Texas, Houston, and Dallas-Fort Worth markets with a 500-mile dispatch radius. The company is SAM.gov registered, holds CAGE Code 170W6, and is a minority-owned business. Nova Titan actively bids fire alarm (Div 28), structured cabling, access control, security camera infrastructure, and communications systems projects for commercial construction and government facilities.
