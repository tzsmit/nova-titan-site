Who We Are

Nova Titan Systems LLC is a professional technology services company based in Lubbock, Texas, serving clients nationwide. We specialize in infrastructure installations, managed IT support, and cybersecurity services for homes, businesses, and government agencies.

As a Black-owned, minority-owned small business, we bring enterprise-grade technical capability with the agility and personalized service of a small company. Our team has experience working inside the systems that power banks, schools, retail stores, and industrial facilities across Texas.

We're SAM.gov registered (UEI: CZ8BLZMSKZ66) and actively pursuing federal contracting opportunities as a Small Disadvantaged Business in West Texas' HUBZone.
