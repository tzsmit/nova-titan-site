Building for the Long Term

From field technician to federal contractor

Traivone Smith founded Nova Titan Systems in 2024 after years of working inside the infrastructure that powers banks, schools, retail environments, and industrial facilities across Texas. He didn't start with funding, a team, or connections—he started with technical skills, a toolbag, and the determination to build something real.

As a Black technologist from West Texas, Traivone saw firsthand how businesses struggled with unreliable IT vendors, slow response times, and surface-level security. He built Nova Titan Systems to solve this problem: delivering enterprise-grade technical execution with the responsiveness and accountability that only a founder-led business can provide.

"I built Nova Titan Systems because communities deserve better technology infrastructure, businesses deserve reliable partners, and the next generation needs to see what ownership looks like."

— Traivone Smith

Today, Nova Titan Systems serves federal agencies, state and local governments, commercial businesses, and industrial facilities with IT infrastructure, cybersecurity, and field engineering services. The company is pursuing federal certifications including SBA 8(a), HUB, and MBE to expand its impact and prove that Black-owned businesses can compete at the highest levels of technical delivery.
