From Rural West Texas to Nationwide Impact

Building technology infrastructure that connects underserved communities to opportunity

Traivone Smith founded Nova Titan Systems in 2024 after years of working inside the infrastructure that powers banks, schools, retail environments, and industrial facilities across Texas. As a Black technologist from rural West Texas, he witnessed firsthand how lack of digital infrastructure limits economic opportunity, education access, and healthcare delivery in underserved communities.

He saw veterans in rural areas struggling with unreliable VA telehealth connections, small-town schools lacking proper cybersecurity protection, and government facilities operating with outdated infrastructure. He built Nova Titan Systems to change that—bringing enterprise-grade IT to the federal, state, and local government agencies serving these communities.

"Technology access is a fundamental right, not a privilege. I built Nova Titan Systems to ensure that veterans in rural areas get the same quality IT infrastructure as those in major cities, that small-town school districts have the cybersecurity they need, and that every government facility we support becomes a bridge to digital opportunity."

— Traivone Smith, Founder & CEO

Today, Nova Titan Systems serves federal-ready government agencies, state governments, and local agencies (cities, counties, school districts) with mission-critical IT infrastructure, delivering services through our prime contractor and subcontractor delivery model. With 95+ projects completed and SAM.gov Active status (UEI: CZ8BLZMSKZ66, CAGE: 170W6), the company is expanding its impact while pursuing additional certifications (Texas HUB, SBA 8(a), MBE) to serve more underserved communities.
