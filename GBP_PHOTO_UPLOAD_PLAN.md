## Overview

This document defines which photos to upload to the Nova Titan Systems Google Business Profile, in what order, with what captions, and why each photo helps GBP trust, relevance, and local prominence.

Upload **8 photos total** across these categories. Do not upload rejected photos (see `rejected_photos_notes.md`).

## After Upload

1. After all 8 photos are uploaded, log in to Google Business Profile and set Photo 1 (warehouse infrastructure) as the **Cover Photo** if Google allows it.
2. Monitor GBP insights after 7–14 days:
   - Check "Photo views" to see which photos get the most impressions
   - Top-viewed photos are your strongest trust signals — keep them updated
3. After completing projects, add 1–2 new field work photos every 60–90 days to keep the profile fresh.
4. Do not remove existing approved photos unless there's a sensitivity issue.
5. Never upload screenshots, marketing graphics, or stock photos to the GBP work/at-work category.
