Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! All plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

What's included in "remote support"?

Remote support includes troubleshooting, software configuration, network diagnostics, security guidance, and general IT assistance via phone, email, or screen sharing.

Do you offer on-site visits?

Yes! Titan Elite includes quarterly on-site visits in West Texas. Other plans can add on-site service at $125/hour + travel (if outside Lubbock area).

What happens if I exceed my monthly hours?

Additional support time is billed at $75/hour (or $60/hour for Pro plan, $50/hour for Elite plan). We'll always notify you before accruing additional charges.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely! You can upgrade anytime, and downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
