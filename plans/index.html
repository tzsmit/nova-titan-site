Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! All plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

What does network monitoring include?

We monitor switch uptime, access point health, firewall status, bandwidth utilization, and latency. You receive automated alerts and regular performance reports.

Do you offer on-site visits?

Yes! Titan Elite includes quarterly on-site infrastructure reviews in West Texas. Other plans can add on-site visits for $250 per visit.

What equipment do you monitor?

We monitor switches, routers, access points, Fortinet firewalls, and other network infrastructure. Device counts are based on your plan tier.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely! You can upgrade anytime, and downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
