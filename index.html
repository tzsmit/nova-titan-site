About Nova Titan Systems

Nova Titan Systems is a founder-led technology services firm based in Lubbock, Texas, serving clients across the United States. Founded by Traivone Smith, we deliver enterprise-grade IT infrastructure, cybersecurity, and managed services with personalized attention and technical expertise.

As a Black-owned, minority-owned small business, we bring a unique perspective and commitment to excellence in every project. We're proud to serve commercial businesses, government agencies, and enterprise partners—delivering reliable technology solutions that drive success.
